Final Debate Motion

Tourism development in Kepulauan Riau should prioritise local Malay culture over modern entertainment attractions.

Choose Your Position

Select one position before preparing your final debate performance.

Final Performance Structure

Use this structure to organise your full response:

  • Opening: State your position clearly.
  • Reason 1: Give your first reason with an example.
  • Reason 2: Give your second reason with an example.
  • Rebuttal: Respond to a possible opposing view.
  • Closing: End with a strong final statement.

Moderator Instruction

Welcome to the final debate performance. Please present a complete debate response based on the motion. You should include your position, reasons, examples, rebuttal, and closing statement.

  • Speak for approximately three minutes.
  • Use examples related to Malay culture, tourism, local identity, traditional arts, local wisdom, or community values in Kepulauan Riau.
  • Use linking words such as first, second, however, for example, therefore, and in conclusion.
  • Try to speak naturally without reading the whole script.
  • Use polite and academic debate language.

Prepare Your Final Debate Notes

Write short notes before speaking. Organise your ideas, but avoid writing a full script.

Final Self-Check and Future AI Feedback

Later, this section can be connected to AI-driven feedback. For now, students can use the checklist below after completing the final performance.

  • Did I state my position clearly?
  • Did I give at least two strong reasons?
  • Did I support my ideas with examples from local culture?
  • Did I include a rebuttal to a possible opposing view?
  • Did I use clear debate structure?
  • Did I speak fluently and confidently?
  • Did I avoid reading the whole script?
Future AI feedback may include clarity, fluency, grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, argument structure, rebuttal quality, confidence, and relevance to Malay culture and tourism in Kepulauan Riau.
