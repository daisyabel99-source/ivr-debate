Reason Structure

Use this structure to make your argument clearer:

  • Position: I agree/disagree with the motion.
  • Reason 1: First, ...
  • Example: For example, ...
  • Reason 2: Another reason is ...
  • Closing: Therefore, ...

Self-Check and Future AI Feedback

Later, this section can be connected to AI-driven feedback. For now, students can use the checklist below after speaking.

  • Did I state my position clearly?
  • Did I give at least two reasons?
  • Did I support my reasons with local culture examples?
  • Did I connect the motion with English learning materials?
  • Did I use linking words such as first, second, for example, and therefore?
  • Did I speak clearly and confidently?
