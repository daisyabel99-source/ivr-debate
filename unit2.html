Debate Motion

Local cultural heritage in Kepulauan Riau should be taught more actively through English learning materials.

Choose Your Position

Select one position before preparing your reasons.

Reason Structure

Use this structure to make your argument clearer:

  • Position: I agree/disagree with the motion.
  • Reason 1: First, ...
  • Example: For example, ...
  • Reason 2: Another reason is ...
  • Closing: Therefore, ...

Moderator Instruction

Welcome to Unit 2. Please present your position and give at least two reasons. Support your reasons with examples from local culture or English learning.

  • State whether you agree or disagree with the motion.
  • Give at least two clear reasons.
  • You may refer to local stories, Malay traditions, traditional arts, local tourism, cultural ceremonies, or daily community life in Kepulauan Riau.
  • Try to connect your reasons with English learning materials.
  • Speak for approximately two minutes.

Prepare Your Reasons

Write short notes before speaking. Focus on your reasons and examples. Do not write a full script.

Self-Check and Future AI Feedback

Later, this section can be connected to AI-driven feedback. For now, students can use the checklist below after speaking.

  • Did I state my position clearly?
  • Did I give at least two reasons?
  • Did I support my reasons with local culture examples?
  • Did I connect the motion with English learning materials?
  • Did I use linking words such as first, second, for example, and therefore?
  • Did I speak clearly and confidently?
Future AI feedback may include clarity, fluency, grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, reason development, examples, and relevance to local cultural heritage.
