Opponent’s Argument

Some people argue that traditional community events are still the best way to preserve local culture because they allow young people to experience cultural values directly, not only through screens.

Rebuttal Structure

Use this structure to respond politely and clearly:

  • Acknowledge: I understand the point that ...
  • Disagree or qualify: However, I believe that ...
  • Reason: This is because ...
  • Evidence or example: For example, ...
  • Closing: Therefore, ...

Self-Check and Future AI Feedback

Later, this section can be connected to AI-driven feedback. For now, students can use the checklist below after speaking.

  • Did I respond to the opponent’s argument?
  • Did I use polite rebuttal language?
  • Did I give a clear reason for my rebuttal?
  • Did I use examples related to local culture or digital media?
  • Did I avoid attacking the speaker personally?
  • Did I speak clearly and confidently?
